Bio

I am a full stack software developer based in Ireland. I earned my chops with the Code Institute. You can see some information about Code Institute and why you could benefit from hiring one of their alumnis here. Prior to becoming a software developer I lived and travelled abroad extensively. I garnered vastly different life and work experiences along the way & I also learned various languages to varying degrees of fluency. I returned home to Ireland after my travels and decided it was time to embark on a career. My love for spoken languages inspired me to learn the langauge of computers and embark on a career in software development.

P.S - I am a big advocate of leaving the world better off than when I came into it. So, if you are browsing here from a charitable or non-profit organization with a lower budget (or perhaps no budget at all) please don't hesitate to get in touch. I am happy to devote time to worthwhile causes.

As a full-stack software developer, I am comfortable in all programming areas, from the frontend to the server. I have a good understanding of UI/UX and can help with all aspects of your project. From concept/brand design to finished product. I design all my projects to be responsive across all devices and also to be completely accesible to the visually impaired. If you have questions about the process then please feel free to ask in the contact section.
